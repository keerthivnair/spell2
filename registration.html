REGISTRATION

The SPELLL-2025 conference will be held in the hybrid mode (both physical and on-line). The authors of accepted papers will have to present their papers on-line, or on site in IIIT Kottayam Campus. The registration fee for main conference will cover the pre-conference workshop and all keynote talks.
